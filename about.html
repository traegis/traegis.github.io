    Traegis Creative (since 2020) is a proprietorship offering services in game development/design, software production, and graphic design. With a focus on merging the opposing forces of design and development in the gaming industry, Traegis has a clear goal to produce high quality gaming experiences through a combination of enticing visual artistic styles and engaging gameplay. Using pixel-art aesthetics, open-world gameplay with decision making elements and fulfilling storlyines, Traegis is looking to make a unique impact to the Indie Gaming genre.
