About the Developer

Traven grew up and currently resides in Vancouver, Canada. Attempting to merge his interests in software development, digital art, and structural engineering in gaming environments with Traegis Creative, Traven has been solely developing Outlandia in his spare time since its conception in 2021 - which was his introduction into the indie gaming space. While his initial educational background was in structural engineering (B.Eng, McGill 2020), he is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science at Simon Fraser University. Traven looks to use his newly developed skills and knowledge of cutting-edge visual computing and machine/deep learning techniques to improve gameplay mechanics, post-processing methods and procedural animations. With a long-term love of the cinematic industry, it is furthermore his interest to utilize his unique background in technical and artistic domains on large-scale projects in either the cinematic arts, or as already mentioned, game development.
