About

Outlandia is a survival western/fantasy Indie RPG. You begin your journey as an experienced bounty hunter looking to move away from the nomadic lifestyle and settle in the booming region of the Isle of Skala. Embrace an open-ended journey filled with crafting and base-building, lush environments, a changing world, decision making, and constant challenges to overcome.

As a wandering bounty hunter (Outland Ranger), you have garnered skills in survival and combat, but long to have a more peaceful life as a rancher. Will you be able to grow a farm on your own, while also keeping your newfound friends from Skala Village safe from a growing evil presence?

Combat

Turn-based combat system

The combat system in OUTLANDIA is similar to that of games such as Pokemon, with a goal of making each battle difficult for the player. Damage is scaled based on armor and weapon stats, player buffs from alchemical potions, and enemy/attack type (dark, light, fire, water, earth, lightning, wind, ice). Battles can also be dynamic in terms of companions - both the player and the current enemy can spawn up to three companion entities to perform random attacks. Player pets will level with the player, and each enemy can be tamed by means of a spell or potion.

Forage

Forage to build up your base and character

To survive in the world of OUTLANDIA, you will be required to forage raw materials from around the map. Health, Stamina and Magik, of which are required for battle (and to not die) do not regenerate automatically. Sleep will regenerate Stamina and Magik. It is therefore necessary to farm, hunt, and fish to have food to go on journeys throughout the world. Magical plants will be located in specific parts of the map, which can be used to create alchemical potions in a cauldron - for use during combat, or to help out villagers in quests.

Farming

Farming is at the basis of every wandering Maegi

Maegi are peaceful humans by nature, and will only use combat in self-defence (they cannot kill neutral animals, without losing their connection to Magik). For this reason, every Maegi must be a proficient farmer in order to survive and provide for villages that fall under their aegis. Farm a variety of crops in OUTLANDIA, for food, but also to sell to villagers for coin - which will allow you to progress in the game.

Crafting

Build structures around the map to customize bases

Structures can be erected around the map, in most locations, once the necessary raw materials have been gathered. These structures will in turn provide shelter, amongst other features (storage, cooking...etc...). The crafting system in OUTLANDIA is based on blueprints, that can be purchased by craftspeople around the world - whereby you can then construct objects by your own means. Cooking is an essential part of crafting, and certain objects that you construct will allow you to combine different food, or magical plants, into delicious foods and alchemical potions. These recipes are always available to the player, provided you can construct the necessary structures used to cook - cauldrons, campfires, ovens...etc...
