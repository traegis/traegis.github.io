Combat

Turn-based combat system

The combat system in OUTLANDIA is similar to that of games such as Pokemon, with a goal of making each battle difficult for the player. Damage is scaled based on armor and weapon stats, player buffs from alchemical potions, and enemy/attack type (dark, light, fire, water, earth, lightning, wind, ice). Battles can also be dynamic in terms of companions - both the player and the current enemy can spawn up to three companion entities to perform random attacks. Player pets will level with the player, and each enemy can be tamed by means of a spell or potion.
