A unique perspective on Indie Gaming

Stylistic Graphics

Making games as they should be through advanced shaders and post-processing - making game more enjoyable with aestheticaly beautiful environments.

Logical Crafting

With a background in structural engineering, building in-game elements and structures has never been more satisfying than in Outlandia.

Emphasize Instincts

Games nowadays hold your hand by forcing you to follow stagnant questlines, or make you use a wiki. What about games where you make up your own mind, without quests - where you make up your own path and maybe learn someting along the way through trial and error!

Real-World Themes

You will never have to question why you are wasting your time playing these games, because it isn't wasting time while you are gaining skills and addressing themes relevant to real-world problems.
