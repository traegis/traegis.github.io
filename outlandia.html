Outlandia is a survival western/fantasy Indie RPG. You begin your journey as an experienced bounty hunter looking to move away from the nomadic lifestyle and settle in the booming region of the Isle of Skala. Embrace an open-ended journey filled with crafting and base-building, lush environments, a changing world, decision making, and constant challenges to overcome.

As a wandering bounty hunter (Outland Ranger), you have garnered skills in survival and combat, but long to have a more peaceful life as a rancher. Will you be able to grow a farm on your own, while also keeping your newfound friends from Skala Village safe from a growing evil presence?

Features

  • Construct customizable ranches -

    Choose to purchase other NPC-run ranches to add to your wealth, or simply develop a quaint farm in an unincorporated region.

  • Forage raw materials -

    As you explore the wilderness of the Isle of Skala. Farm regular and magical crops for unique foods and powers.

  • Combat a wide range of enemies -

    Use a mix of stealth, ranged and melee attack formats to combat fantasy-themed monsters that appear mysteriously throughout the night, and ill-willed humans alike.

  • Gain companions throughout your saga -

    Have the option to form romantic relationships with certain NPCs, and purchase pets and farm animals for your benefit. Use hired guns from the village to help you dispose of threats around the region.
