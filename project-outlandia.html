Outlandia

A sandbox building and exploration gaming experience like never before. Do you have what it takes to not just survive, but build a new home for yourself, alone in the wildnerness of British-Columbia, Canada?

View Steam Page

Use your instincts as a survivalist to coexist with nature in the world of Outlandia. All resources taken from the Earth will have direct implications in the form of harsher weather, shorter farming seasons, and general loss of flora and fauna due to ecosystem imbalance. Develop your skills as an engineer with your BLDNet drone companion, which allows you to 3D print a wide and evolving set of structural elements to construct a home base that caters to your wants and needs - but make sure to check for structural stability lest you lose all progress!

Play as a survivalist who, in the year 2035, grows tired of their mundane city-suburb existence and decides to utilize newly developed futuristic building tools to their advantage to craft a new life as a hermit in the wild. With a clear inspiration from "Into the Wild" by John Krakauer, explore an ever-changing environment reminiscent of the British-Columbian wilderness. Can the wild adapt to your presence as well as you adapt to its?
